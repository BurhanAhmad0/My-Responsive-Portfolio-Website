About

I am a skilled web developer specializing in creating stunning websites using pure HTML, CSS and JavaScript. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for clean, efficient code, I design websites that are not only visually appealing but also user-friendly and fast-loading. Whether you need a simple landing page, a personal blog, or a complete website overhaul, I am here to bring your vision to life.
